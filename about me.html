Who Am I

I'm the weird kid from high school. I never really had a lot of friends. I was never out with friends, never talking to anyone, I wasn't even that smart. I was alone, and that was becuase people really didn't like me. It wasn't that I was too nerdy, I wasn't smart enough for that. It wasn't becuase I was too mean, I always tried to be nice to others. It was becuase I was different. They liked watching Netflix shows, I liked watching Anime on Crunchyroll. They liked playing basketball and football, I liked soccer. They talked about going cross country or international for vacation. I talked about visiting my family. Since I was different, I got hurt. I ended up becoming resentful becuase of it. In all honesty, I was jealous at the time. I wanted lots of friends and I wanted to be accepted for who I was, but I wasn't. So I did the next best thing. I took the pain, used it to improve myself and left everyone else in the dust. Now, I watch them. The things they criticized me for in the past are becoming popular. Now, they're starting to copy who I was years ago. Yet here I am, waiting to see if they change their minds once again. Am I still resentful? A little bit. But I know who my friends are. I know what I like, and who I am. I am the weird kid, and I'm proud of it.
